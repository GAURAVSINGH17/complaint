Our Principles

Core Values That Drive Us

The foundation of trust and excellence in grievance redressal

Transparency

Every step of the grievance process is visible to the complainant. No hidden procedures, no black boxes - complete clarity in how your case is handled.

Efficiency

Time is critical. Our automated workflows and smart routing ensure your grievance reaches the right department within minutes, not days.

Security

Bank-grade 256-bit encryption protects your data. Your identity and grievance details are confidential and secure at every step.

Empathy

Behind every grievance is a human story. We train our officers to listen, understand, and resolve with compassion and respect.

Fairness

Every case is judged on its merit. Our appeal system ensures that if you're unsatisfied, you have the right to a second review.

Innovation

We continuously evolve our platform using AI and data analytics to predict bottlenecks and improve resolution times.

How It Works

Our Grievance Resolution Process

A streamlined journey from submission to resolution

Submit Your Grievance

Fill out our user-friendly digital form with details of your issue. Attach supporting documents, photos, or videos. Receive an instant acknowledgment with a unique tracking ID.

1
2

Smart Routing & Assignment

Our AI-powered system automatically categorizes and routes your grievance to the appropriate department. Senior officers review complex cases for priority handling.

Investigation & Resolution

Assigned officers investigate your case, gather facts, and work towards resolution. You receive real-time updates at every stage via SMS and email notifications.

3
4

Closure & Feedback

Once resolved, you receive a detailed closure report. Rate the resolution and provide feedback to help us improve. Dissatisfied? Escalate for review within 30 days.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

From inception to becoming India's trusted grievance platform

2020

Platform Launch

Code Flow was launched as a pilot project in New Delhi with 5 government departments integrated.

2021

National Expansion

Expanded to 15 states, integrated AI-based routing system, and crossed 50,000 resolved grievances.

2022

Mobile App Launch
