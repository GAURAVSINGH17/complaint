Guidelines for Students

Important policies, rules, and procedures for grievance handling

Important Information
These guidelines are mandatory for all students. Please read them carefully before submitting any grievance. Violation of these rules may result in rejection of your complaint.
Grievance Submission Rules
4 Rules
1

Valid Complaint Format

All grievances must be submitted via the official portal with a valid student ID. Anonymous submissions will not be entertained.

2

Evidence Requirement

Students must attach relevant screenshots, documents, or photos to support their claim. Complaints without evidence may be marked as invalid.

3

One Issue Per Ticket

Please raise separate tickets for separate issues. Combining multiple unrelated problems in one ticket causes delays.

4

Respectful Communication

Use professional language. Abusive or threatening language will result in immediate rejection of the ticket and possible disciplinary action.

Response & Resolution Time
3 Rules
1

Initial Acknowledgment

An automated acknowledgment will be sent within 1 hour of submission. If not received, check your spam folder.

2

Resolution Timeline

Standard grievances will be resolved within 5-7 working days. Complex issues may take up to 15 working days.

3

Escalation Window

If no response is received within the stipulated time, students may escalate the issue to the Department Head.

Privacy & Confidentiality
2 Rules
1

Data Protection

All personal data submitted is encrypted and stored securely. It will only be accessed by authorized personnel.

2

Non-Disclosure

The identity of the complainant will be kept confidential unless disclosure is required by law or for investigation purposes.
