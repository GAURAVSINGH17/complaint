Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about filing grievances, tracking status, and using the Code Flow platform

Home / FAQs
What is Code Flow Digital Grievance Portal?

Code Flow is a Digital Complaint and Grievance Analytical System designed for students to submit, track, and resolve grievances efficiently. It provides a transparent platform where you can:

  • File complaints across multiple categories (Academic, Administrative, Hostel, etc.)
  • Track real-time status updates
  • Communicate with administration through the mail system
  • View insights and analytics on grievance resolution
  • Access guidelines and important notices
General
Is my complaint data secure and confidential?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and data security:

  • Bank-grade 256-bit encryption protects all your data
  • Only authorized administrators can view your complaint details
  • Your personal information is never shared with third parties
  • Sensitive grievances (harassment, ragging) are handled with strict confidentiality
  • Secure login with session management
General
How do I register on the portal?

Registration is simple and takes just 2 minutes:

  • Click on "Register" in the top navigation
  • Enter your institutional email address
  • Create a secure password (min 8 characters with numbers)
  • Verify your email through the OTP sent to your inbox
  • Complete your profile with basic details (Name, Roll Number, Department)

Note: You must use your university/institution email (@yourcollege.edu) to register.

Account
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

If you've forgotten your password:

  • Go to the Login page
  • Click on "Forgot Password?" below the login form
  • Enter your registered email address
  • Check your email for the password reset link (valid for 1 hour)
  • Create a new strong password

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact technical support.

Account
How do I submit a new grievance?

Submitting a grievance is straightforward:

  • Login to your dashboard and click "Submit Grievance"
  • Select the appropriate Category (Academic, Hostel, Transport, etc.)
  • Fill in the subject line with a brief description
  • Provide detailed information in the description box
  • Upload supporting documents (images, PDFs) if available
  • Review and click Submit

You'll immediately receive a Grievance ID (e.g., ACD001xyz@12) - save this for tracking!

Submission
What categories of grievances can I file?

Code Flow supports 12 complaint categories:

  • Academic: Curriculum, faculty, examinations, grading issues
  • Hostel Facilities: Rooms, mess, sanitation, amenities
  • Transport & Community: Bus services, commuting issues
  • IT & Digital Services: WiFi, portals, software issues
  • Library Services: Books, digital resources, facility issues
  • Administrative: Admission, documents, general admin
  • Financial: Fees, scholarships, payments
  • Placement & Career: Internships, recruitment drives
  • Harassment/Ragging: Strictly confidential handling
  • And more specialized categories...
Submission
What file types and sizes are supported for attachments?

You can attach supporting documents with these specifications:

  • Formats: Images (JPG, PNG, GIF), Documents (PDF, DOC, DOCX), Text files (TXT)
  • Size Limit: Maximum 10MB per file
  • Multiple Files: You can upload up to 5 files per grievance
  • Recommended: Screenshots for technical issues, Bills for financial grievances, Photos for facility issues

Tip: Ensure images are clear and documents are readable before uploading.

Submission
How can I track the status of my complaint?

You can track your grievance in multiple ways:

  • Dashboard: Login to see all your grievances with current status cards
  • My Grievances: View detailed list with category-wise organization
  • Check Status Tool: Enter your Grievance ID to get instant updates
  • Email Notifications: Receive automatic updates when status changes

Status Meanings:
🟡 Pending: Submitted, awaiting review
🔵 Under Review: Being examined by relevant department
🟢 Resolved: Solution implemented
🔴 Escalated: Moved to higher authorities

Tracking
How long does it take to resolve a grievance?

Resolution time varies by category and complexity:

  • Simple Issues: 24-48 hours (IT support, document requests)
  • Standard Issues: 3-5 working days (Academic queries, facility repairs)
  • Complex Issues: 7-14 working days (Administrative changes, policy matters)
  • Investigation Required: 15-30 days (Harassment, serious disciplinary matters)

SLA: We commit to providing a first response within 24 hours and weekly progress updates until resolution.

Tracking
What if I'm not satisfied with the resolution?

If you're not satisfied with the resolution:

  • You can Reopen the grievance within 7 days of closure with additional comments
  • File an Appeal through the "Appeal Decision" option in your dashboard
  • Escalate to senior authorities by selecting "Escalate" option
  • Contact the Grievance Cell directly through the Mail system

Every appeal is reviewed by a senior officer not involved in the original decision to ensure fairness.

Resolution
Can I communicate with the officer handling my case?

Yes! Two-way communication is available:

  • Mail System: Access the internal messaging system from your dashboard
  • Comments: Add comments to your grievance thread anytime
  • Notifications: Officers may request additional info via email/SMS
  • Meetings: For complex issues, you can request a video call appointment

All communications are logged and attached to your grievance record for transparency.

Resolution
The website is not loading / I'm facing technical issues. What should I do?

If you encounter technical problems:

  • Clear Cache: Clear browser cache and cookies, then reload
  • Browser: Use updated Chrome, Firefox, or Safari for best experience
  • Mobile: Enable desktop mode if mobile site has issues
  • Support Form: Click the "Support" button in your dashboard
  • Emergency: Email technical support at support@codeflow.gov

System Requirements: Internet Explorer is not supported. Use Edge, Chrome, Firefox, or Safari updated within last 2 years.
